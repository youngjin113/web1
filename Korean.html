우리동네에서 찾을 수 있는 언어교환 모임

Nature Seychelles is selling NFTs of the Seychelles magpie robin to support its work as it has lost other funding during the coronavirus pandemic.

The 59 NFTs are being sold for between $10 and $10,000 each. Nirmal Shah, CEO of Nature Seychelles, told AFP that the group already raised $2,500 on the first day of sales.
